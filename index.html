    [github]

    The main purpose of the application would be to become a classroom necessity for students/professors. There are three primary parts to this project:

    1. The app flow for attendance would be the professor sets a time range for attendance (i.e. first five minutes of class) and a location (i.e. Moffitt), and the student must be present in that location in order to sign into attendance.
